10.1 A psychologist has selected 12 handicap workers randomly from production workers employed at various factories in a large industrial complex and their work competency scores are examined as follows: The psychologist wants to test whether the population average score is 45. Assume the population distribution is symmetrical about the mean.

32, 52, 21, 39, 23, 55, 36, 27, 37, 41, 34, 51

1) Check whether a parametric test is possible.
2) Apply the sign test with the significance level of 5%.
3) Apply the Wilcoxon signed rank test with the significance level of 5%.
