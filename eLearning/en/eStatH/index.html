Statistics education is very essential for elementary and middle school students who will lead the big data society in the future. But, there has been no appropriate educational software for stuendents. „ÄéeStatH„Äè is a free web-based software which is designed specially to study the statistics of high school so that students can easily learn and practice statistics anytime, anywhere on the Internet.

This web-book is linked with „ÄéeStatH„Äè, so you can practice easily as well as studying statistics. I hope you become a talented person who understand statistics by using this software and leads the future big data society.
